Discography

Albums that shaped Westlife’s global success

Westlife
1999
Popular Song: Flying Without Wings

This debut album introduced Westlife to the world and showcased their emotional ballads and harmonies. “Flying Without Wings” became an inspirational anthem and helped establish their global fanbase.

Coast to Coast
2000
Popular Song: My Love

“My Love” became one of Westlife’s signature songs, loved for its heartfelt lyrics and melodic chorus. The album strengthened their presence across Europe and Asia.

World of Our Own
2001
Popular Song: World of Our Own

This album marked a more confident and mature sound for the group. The title track became a fan favorite for its uplifting message and strong pop appeal.

Turnaround
2002
Popular Song: Unbreakable

Featuring powerful ballads and refined production, “Unbreakable” resonated with fans for its emotional strength and became a staple in Westlife’s live performances.

Spectrum
2019
Popular Song: Hello My Love

After their comeback, “Spectrum” proved Westlife’s lasting relevance. “Hello My Love” blended modern pop with classic Westlife emotion, winning over old and new fans alike.
