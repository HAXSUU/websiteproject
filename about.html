About Westlife

Westlife is an Irish pop vocal group that has captured the hearts of millions around the world with timeless ballads, emotional performances, and unforgettable harmonies. Formed in 1998, the group quickly rose to global fame and became one of the most successful pop acts of all time.

The group consists of Shane Filan, Mark Feehily, Kian Egan, and Nicky Byrne. Known for their powerful vocals and meaningful lyrics, Westlife has released numerous chart-topping albums and singles, including iconic songs such as Flying Without Wings, You Raise Me Up, and My Love.

With millions of records sold worldwide, countless awards, and sold-out tours across continents, Westlife remains a symbol of passion, dedication, and musical excellence.
