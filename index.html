About Westlife

Westlife is an Irish pop vocal group known for timeless ballads, iconic harmonies, and unforgettable hits such as You Raise Me Up, Flying Without Wings, and My Love. With millions of albums sold worldwide, they remain one of the most beloved pop groups of all time.
